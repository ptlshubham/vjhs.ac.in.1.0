V. J. Patel Higher Secondary School
run by Charotar Education Society was established in 2008. This school is named after Pujya Vitthalbhai Sahib(Vitthalbhai Jethabhai Patel). The first achievement of this school was in the year 2010, in the first batch of the board, it got the name by getting excellent results in the entire district and the state. In which, one to eight numbers were obtained by the school in the district and the first two numbers in the state.

Vision

  • To facilitate transformation of students into good human beings, responsible citizens and competent professionals, focusing on assimilation, generation and dissemination of knowledge.
  • To impart quality higher education and focus on research and innovation to cater to the need of the country.

Mission

  • Impart quality education to meet the needs of profession and society, and achieve excellence in teaching-learning and research.
  • To impart quality scientific education and produce postgraduates, technologists, scientists and citizens who will contribute meaningfully to the growth and development of the country and excel in various disciplines of knowledge.
  • To create a research-oriented teaching-learning environment in the Institute with a focus on excellence and innovation.
