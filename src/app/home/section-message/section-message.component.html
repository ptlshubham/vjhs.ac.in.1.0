Message From Principal's desk

Dr. Bhavesh Dave
- Principal, M.B.Patel Science College

Greetings to all eager students of M.B.Patel Science Institution, operated by Charotar Education Society, reaccredited with a 'A' grade by NAAC, accredited with a 'A' grade by KCG, and recognised by UGC as a college with promise. The institution, which was founded in 1967, is one of the oldest and most prestigious in the region, with competent and committed faculty, enough facilities and infrastructure for academic and extra-curricular activities.

The college provides twelve undergrad courses as well as a postgraduate diploma and certificate in Medical Laboratory Technology. The college is a Ph.D.-granting institution in Chemistry, Biochemistry, and Food & Nutrition. The institution offers courses in skill development such as SCOPE, DELL, and Personality Development. The institute supports a variety of extracurricular activities such as NCC, NSS, and sports. Students are also taught about their social duties through activities such as blood donation, tree care and planting, free eye check-up camps, and AIDS awareness programmes, to name a few.
